Magic Box

Anonymous Voice Platform for Youth

Magic Box is a safe and anonymous space where students can share thoughts they may not feel comfortable discussing with friends or family.

Purpose

  • Support honest and anonymous self-expression.
  • Create a judgment-free student community.
  • Help students feel seen, heard, and understood.
  • Build empathy through shared experiences.

Current Status

The pilot program is running across all three high school grade levels.

Future expansion is being explored through the Root & Shoot Global Network.

Harmony Bridge

Music-Based Emotional Learning for Neurodiverse Children

Harmony Bridge is a nonprofit concept focused on supporting neurodiverse children through music-based emotional learning.

Approach

  • Use handpan music to encourage calm and connection.
  • Use classical music to support emotional recognition.
  • Design programs for different ages and abilities.
  • Develop family support and educator resources.

Current Status

The project is temporarily paused while resources, partnerships, and implementation plans are being developed.
